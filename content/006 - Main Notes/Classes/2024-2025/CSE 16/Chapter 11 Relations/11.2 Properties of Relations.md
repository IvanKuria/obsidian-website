#  Properties of Relations

- Suppose $R$ is a relation on a [[set]] $A$.
## Reflexive
- Relation $R$ is reflexive if $xRx$ for every $x Ôłł A$. That is, $R$ is reflexive if $ÔłÇ x Ôłł A, xRx$

## Symmetric
- Relation $R$ is symmetric if $xRy$ implies $yRx$ for all $x, y Ôłł A$. That is, $R$ is symmetric if $ÔłÇ x, y Ôłł A, xR y Ôçĺ yRx$.

## Transitive
- Relation $R$ is transitive if whenever $xRy$ and $yRz$, then also $xRz$. That is, $R$ is transitive if $ÔłÇ x, y, z Ôłł A, ((xRy) Ôłž (yRz)) Ôçĺ xRz$
