# *“We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit.”*

I like this quote not because it’s deep,  
but because it’s kind of annoying.

Because it means change isn’t always about big moments which I love. That rush of dopamine is addictive.  
It’s about what you do when no one’s watching.  
On random Thursdays.  
On boring weeks.
