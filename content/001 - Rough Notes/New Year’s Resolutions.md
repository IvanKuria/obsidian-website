Every January, we kinda do the same thing.  
We tell ourselves we’ll change 
Work out more, focus harder, fix everything. Code more(me!)

And for a few days… maybe a week…  
it kind of works.

Then life happens.  
You miss a day.  
Then two.  
Then suddenly it’s February, and the goal just… disappears.

Not in a some flashy or dramatic way.  
No big failure moment.  
You just stop thinking about it.
