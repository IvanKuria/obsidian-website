## Introduction


Stellarize is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.


---


## Information We Collect


### Information You Provide


- **Email Address**: Used for account authentication and communication

- **Username**: Your chosen display name within the app

- **Avatar Selection**: Your chosen profile icon


### Automatically Collected Information


- **Focus Session Data**: Duration, activity type, and timestamps of your focus sessions

- **Progress Data**: Planet unlock status, streak counts, and achievement progress

- **Usage Statistics**: Daily focus minutes, total minutes, and activity breakdowns

- **Device Information**: Timezone and region settings for accurate time tracking


---


## How We Use Your Information

  

We use the collected information to:

  

- Provide and maintain the app's core functionality

- Track your focus sessions and progress

- Calculate streaks and achievements

- Display personalized statistics and heatmaps

- Sync your data across devices

- Process in-app purchases and subscriptions

- Send optional notifications for reminders and streak maintenance

  

---

  

## Data Storage and Security

  

- Your data is securely stored using **Supabase** (cloud database infrastructure)

- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS

- We implement authentication and authorization to protect your account

- Only you have access to your personal focus data

## Your Data Rights

  

You have the right to:

  

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data

- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data

- **Correction**: Update or correct your personal information

- **Export**: Download your focus session history and statistics

  

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below or use the "Delete Account" option in the app settings.

  

---

  

## Data Retention

  

- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained as long as your account is active

- **Deleted Accounts**: All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

- **Session History**: Focus session history is retained to provide historical statistics and heatmaps
