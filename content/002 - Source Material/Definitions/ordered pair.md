---
title: "Ordered Pair"
type: definition
domain: discrete-math
aliases: [ordered pairs]
tags:
  - definition
---

A list $(x, y)$ of two things $x$ and $y$, enclosed in parentheses and separated by a comma. Unlike a set, order matters: $(a, b) \neq (b, a)$ unless $a = b$.
