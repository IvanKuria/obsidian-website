---
title: "Division Algorithm"
type: definition
domain: discrete-math
aliases: [division algorithm]
tags:
  - definition
---

Given integers $a$ and $b$ with $b > 0$, there exist unique integers $q$ and $r$ for which $a = qb + r$ and $0 \leq r < b$.
