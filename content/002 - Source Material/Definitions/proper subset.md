title: "Proper Subset"
type: definition
domain: discrete-math
aliases: [proper subsets, strict subset]
tags:
  - definition
---

Set $A$ is a proper subset of $B$ if $A \subseteq B$ and $A \neq B$. Written as $A \subset B$. This means every element of $A$ is in $B$, but $B$ contains at least one element not in $A$.
