---
title: "Open Sentence"
type: definition
domain: discrete-math
aliases: [open sentences, predicate]
tags:
  - definition
---

A sentence whose truth value depends on the value of one or more variables. For example, "$x > 5$" is an open sentence because its truth depends on the value assigned to $x$.
