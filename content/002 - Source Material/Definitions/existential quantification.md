A logical quantifier expressed as:
$$\exists x \in X, \space P(x)$$
Meaning "there exists an $x$ in $X$ such that $P(x)$ is true." Analogous to a logical disjunction ($\bigvee$) over all elements.

## Context

Existential quantification is one of two fundamental quantifiers in predicate logic (the other being [[universal quantification]]). For example, $\exists x \in \mathbb{Z}, x^2 = 4$ is true because $x = 2$ satisfies it.
