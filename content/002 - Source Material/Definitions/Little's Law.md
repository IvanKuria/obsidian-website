---
title: "Little's Law"
type: definition
domain: operating-systems
aliases: []
tags:
  - definition
---

States that the average number of items in a stable system ($L$) equals their average arrival rate ($\lambda$) multiplied by their average time in the system ($W$): $L = \lambda W$.
