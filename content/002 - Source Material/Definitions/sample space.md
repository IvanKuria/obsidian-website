The set of all possible outcomes of an experiment, typically denoted $S$ or $\Omega$.

## Context

The sample space defines the universe of possibilities for a probability experiment. For example, flipping a coin has sample space $S = \{H, T\}$, while rolling a die has $S = \{1,2,3,4,5,6\}$. Every [[event]] is a subset of the sample space.
